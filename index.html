Porta Potty Rental FAQ's

Anyone can rent a porta potty! Whether you're a homeowner planning a backyard event, a contractor managing a construction site, or an event organizer coordinating a large festival, we provide portable restroom solutions for all needs.

We offer flexible rental periods from daily to monthly rentals. Whether you need a porta potty for a single day event or a long-term construction project lasting several months, we can accommodate your timeline.

Rent a porta potty whenever you have an outdoor event, construction project, or any situation where permanent restroom facilities are unavailable or insufficient. Popular occasions include weddings, festivals, concerts, construction sites, and outdoor parties.

Service frequency depends on usage and rental duration. For events, units are thoroughly cleaned before delivery. For long-term rentals, we typically service units weekly, but we can adjust the schedule based on your specific needs and usage levels.

Standard porta potty rentals include the unit itself, toilet paper, hand sanitizer, delivery, setup, and pickup. Deluxe units may include additional amenities like flushing capabilities, sinks, mirrors, and better ventilation.

We recommend reserving your porta potty as soon as you know your event date or project start time. While we offer same-day delivery when available, booking in advance ensures you get the exact units you need, especially during busy seasons.
