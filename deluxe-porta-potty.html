About Our Deluxe Porta Potties

Take your event or project to the next level with our deluxe porta potties, designed to provide superior comfort and convenience compared to standard units. These upgraded portable restrooms are perfect for weddings, corporate events, upscale gatherings, and any occasion where guest comfort and satisfaction are priorities. The deluxe model combines functionality with enhanced amenities to create a more pleasant restroom experience.

What sets our deluxe porta potties apart is the built-in handwashing station featuring a foot-pump operated sink that provides fresh, running water for proper hand hygiene. Each unit also includes a soap dispenser with premium hand soap, a paper towel dispenser for drying, a mirror for grooming, and a convenient shelf for personal items. The interior is more spacious than standard units, with improved ventilation systems that maintain a fresh, comfortable environment throughout your rental period.

These units maintain the same durable construction as our standard models while adding premium touches that your guests will appreciate. The deluxe porta potty is an excellent middle-ground option, offering significant comfort upgrades at a moderate price point compared to luxury restroom trailers, making it ideal for event planners who want to impress guests without breaking the budget.

Planning Tips

How Many Units Do You Need?

General guidelines for deluxe porta potty quantities:

  • Up to 50 guests: 1-2 deluxe units
  • 50-100 guests: 2-3 deluxe units
  • 100-150 guests: 3-4 deluxe units
  • 150+ guests: Add 1 unit per 50 additional guests

For events with alcohol service, consider adding 20% more units. For events longer than 4 hours, additional units may be beneficial.
