About Our Standard Porta Potties

Our standard porta potties are the most economical and popular choice for construction sites, outdoor events, festivals, and temporary facilities throughout Franklin, Tennessee. Built with durable fiberglass construction, these units are designed to withstand the elements while providing reliable restroom facilities for your workers, guests, or attendees.

Each standard porta potty comes equipped with essential amenities including a non-slip floor for safety, a self-closing door with secure latch for privacy, adequate ventilation system to maintain freshness, an interior hook for hanging personal items, and a built-in hand sanitizer dispenser. The translucent roof allows natural light during the day, reducing the need for battery-powered lighting.

These units are serviced and maintained on a regular schedule, with thorough cleaning, restocking of supplies, and tank pumping as needed. Our professional service team ensures each unit meets our high standards of cleanliness and functionality before delivery and throughout your rental period.
